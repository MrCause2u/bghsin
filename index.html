FAQs about Sponsorship

Common questions about child sponsorship with Aloysius Ministries

Where can I sponsor a child?

You can sponsor a child through Aloysius Ministries in Kyenjojo, Uganda. Our children are located in GR2R+87 Lwebali, Kyenjojo - Uganda, where they live in challenging conditions and need your support for education, meals, and basic needs.

How much of my $50 monthly gift actually goes to my sponsored child?

100% of your $50 monthly sponsorship goes directly to supporting your sponsored child and our children's program. This covers their education, daily meals, medical care, Bible study materials, and other essential needs. We are committed to transparency and ensuring every dollar makes a direct impact.

How long will my sponsorship last?

Your sponsorship can last as long as you wish to continue supporting your sponsored child. Most sponsorships continue until the child completes their education or becomes self-sufficient. You can modify or end your sponsorship at any time by contacting us at support@aloysiusministries.org.

What if I need to make a change to my sponsorship?

We understand that circumstances can change. You can easily modify your sponsorship by contacting our support team at support@aloysiusministries.org or calling +256 787 833 539. We'll work with you to find a solution that works for both you and your sponsored child.

Who is Aloysius Ministries?

Aloysius Ministries is a life-changing Christian ministry founded by Pastor Kabagambe Aloyzious, dedicated to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ and helping children in need. We are located in Kyenjojo, Uganda, and serve over 250 children through our Bible study, education, and sponsorship programs.

I received a special code from a concert recently. How can I use that code to start sponsoring?

If you received a special sponsorship code from one of our ministry events or concerts, please email us at support@aloysiusministries.org with your code and we'll help you start sponsoring a child immediately. Include the code in your email subject line for faster processing.
